Howling Wastes Garden

After days of walking through the wasteland you assume you are seeing a hallucination. In the dying light, the horizon presents a huge structure with... light! They have power! You hurry forward with hope and renewed spring in your step.
A minute later, you find a sapling growing - 🌱 ! Interesting... hardly anything grows after the great Sea Blooms began.

As you finally approach the gates of the huge walls, a small holo sign catches your eye. NOTICE: Jawas have been sighted in the area, all travelers are advised to use caution. They have already stolen all of the text links and replaced them with images! A Jawa is shown in the piercing detail and vibrant colors of the holo, which is somehow more real to you than the world around you.
